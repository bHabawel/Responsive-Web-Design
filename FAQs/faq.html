What’s the difference between Single Origin Coffee and Commercial Coffee?

Single-origin is a phrase used to let the people know the coffees are sourced from a single producer or region in one country. We took this term and used it to let you know the producer, farm, cooperative, and region. Only from these producers with quality coffee processing (harvest and post-harvest) are chosen. Our single-origin coffee also provides you the coffee traceability. This includes growing altitude, coffee variety, and coffee process. The coffee roasting is an artisan and small batch for better and even roast. With the meticulous process and coffee quality, you get a delicious cup of coffee but it is expected to have a higher price.
Our commercial coffee is your quick caffeine fix. Coffee cherries are strip-picked, same coffee variety but sourced from different regions, varietal and cultivar are unknown. Coffee cup quality is not as good as our Single Origin Coffee. Commercial coffee is roasted in bulk.
